#### Summary
Utilised the complete 12 steps of the Luring Communication Theory (LCT) to investigate differences between contact-driven and fantasy-driven offenders in chat transcripts (including both PJ and law enforcement data). 

#### Key Points
This approach reinforced the existence and utility of the motivational distinction between offender types, revealing clear differences in how they utilised various LCT steps to exploit perceived child victims.
